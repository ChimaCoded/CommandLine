convey verb
con·vey | \ kən-ˈvā \
conveyed; conveying

transitive verb

1a : to bear from one place to another 
     especially: to move in a continuous 
     stream or mass

 b : to impart or communicate by statement, 
     suggestion, gesture, or appearance 
   // struggling to convey his feelings

 c : to transfer or deliver (something, such 
     as property) to another especially by 
     a sealed writing

 d : to cause to pass from one place or person 
     to another
   // convey a message
